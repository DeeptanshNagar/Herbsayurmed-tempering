About Herbsayurmed

Founded in 1998 and registered in 2011, Herbsayurmed Pharmaceuticals has gained immense expertise in supplying & trading of Ayurvedic medicines. Based in Rudrapur, Uttarakhand, India, we are one of the oldest and most trusted firms in the industry.

Under our brand name INDIAN'S, we offer premium Ayurvedic herbal products including Ajmodh Ras, Carlee Cal, Giloy Ras, Ajmodh Extract, and Hepadyn Syrup. All our products are non-toxic, highly effective, and made from high-quality herbs grown naturally without pesticides, insecticides, herbicides, or chemical fertilizers.

With 27 years of legacy and quality experience, we have served over 100,000 happy customers through 200+ retail outlets across India, maintaining our commitment to 100% authentic Ayurveda.
